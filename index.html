ABOUT THE TEAM

Key features of our Team

Responsibility

The work is supported by a sense of responsibility towards society in making it more inclusive, close to the needs of all.

Passion

We are a bunch of passionate folks working to increase penetration of education in those parts of the world where it’s tough to access.

Outreach

We are a team from 7 different countries collaborating with each other and all working for the same cause.

Support

To Support all ethnicities and people around the globe for sustainable development.

Know our Project.

All of us have been part of the revered Millennium Campus Network and have been awarded with MCN Fellowship.

The project was born in a multicultural environment with the aim of making educational content more accessible through the use of social media so that children around the globe could access the content 24 X 7 and gain valuable insights.
